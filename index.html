Information about Company

Who We are?

VITTORIO is an Italian company that creates perfume compositions of the highest perfumery and aesthetic value.

Our fragrances are based on ingredients grown on our own plantations in the cradle of world perfumery, French Grasse and the Italian commune of Moretta, a few kilometers from Turin.

Information about Company

Why We are Special

The company was established in 2022 by a team of professionals working in the perfume industry for over 40 years.

As newcomers to the fragrance industry, we challenge old rules and adopt a flexible, customer-centric approach. Experienced perfumers bring to life the ideas and dreams of clients in an impeccable way.

Information about Company

Why We are Ordinary

With unending passion, we innovate to always stay at the forefront of market trends.

An advanced research laboratory, combined with the vast experience of our specialists, allows us to bring any ideas to life. High-tech robotic modern production of fragrances for perfumery and households allows to produce up to 10,000 kg of products per day.

PRODUCTION

Our innovative robotic production allows you to produce products from 1 kg to several tons. In order to realize the ideas of our customers without the slightest deviation, we use equipment with the highest reliability, productivity and, most importantly, accuracy and error-free dosing of ingredients.
We have all the necessary international certificates, and the products are safe and meet all international standards.

Our best Fragrances

Fine Fragrance is our passion and mission. With a huge database of fragrance formulas, we are always researching the market and are on the current wave of modern trends.

INGREDIENTS

On our own plantations in France and Italy with an area of 160 hectares, we grow and process our own absolutes, resinoids, rectified and atherpene oils, aromatic and semi-synthetic products. Own natural raw materials of the highest quality from the fertile lands of Southern Europe give our fragrances originality and sophistication.

Our best R&D

Our laboratory is equipped with all necessary equipment. Here we create new fragrances and make sure that the quality of our products and the raw materials used is always at the highest level.

Maintaining consistently high product quality is a priority for the quality control department of the laboratory. GC-MS systems, physico-chemical and olfactory analyzes, as well as an impressive 40 years of experience, allow us to confirm the quality of all our products.
